About Us

Founded in 2001, Studio 88 has grown into one of South Africa's leading fashion retailers. We specialize in providing international sports and lifestyle brands at prices that are affordable for everyone. With over 300 stores nationwide, we are proud to bring the latest fashion trends closer to our customers in every corner of the country. Our stores are designed to create an enjoyable and seamless shopping experience for every visitor.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, trendy apparel, footwear, and accessories while ensuring that our customers receive exceptional service at every step. We strive to cater to a wide range of styles and preferences, from sports enthusiasts and casual wearers to fashion-forward trendsetters. At Studio 88, we believe that everyone deserves access to fashionable clothing and footwear without compromising on quality or breaking the bank.

We are also committed to supporting local communities and contributing positively to the South African retail landscape. Our team continuously works on curating collections that reflect both global trends and local tastes, ensuring our customers find something unique at every visit.

Our Experience

Over the years, Studio 88 has grown into a nationwide brand recognized for its consistent quality, variety, and customer satisfaction. With a presence in major cities and towns, our stores offer a welcoming and engaging environment where customers can explore our wide range of products.

Our experience extends beyond just retail; we are constantly innovating in our product offerings, store layouts, and online platforms to provide an effortless shopping experience. We combine fashion expertise with customer insight to ensure that our collections meet the evolving demands of our clientele.
