About Us

Studio 88 has been established in 2001, Studio 88 is a leading South African fashion retailer, offering international sports and lifestyle brands at affordable prices. The company has grown into a nationwide chain with over 300 stores. Our mission is to provide customers with high-quality, trendy apparel and footwear while ensuring excellent customer service. We pride ourselves on our diverse product range that caters to various styles and preferences. At Studio 88, we believe in creating a shopping experience that is both enjoyable and accessible, making fashion attainable for everyone.
